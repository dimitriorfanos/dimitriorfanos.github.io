About

Engineer. Builder. Problem-solver.

I'm a process and R&D engineer with ~3.5 years of experience spanning R&D, systems test, instrumentation & controls, and thermal engineering. My work lives at the intersection of chemistry, plasma physics, and the hardware systems that bring them to scale.

Currently at MAAT Energy, I develop and operate pilot-scale microwave plasma reactors for syngas, hydrogen, and carbon production — and build the SCADA systems that run them reliably day-to-day.

I care about building things that actually work: instrumented, robust, and ready to scale.
