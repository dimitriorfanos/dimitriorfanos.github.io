Problem
High reflected power was limiting plasma performance, and the relationship between stub tuning and impedance transformation was not well understood.
Approach
Self-directed study of matching networks, S-parameters, and Smith chart methods. Modeled 3- and 4-stub tuner behavior in CST Studio Suite to develop a systematic tuning methodology.
Outcome
Reduced reflected power from 30% to <1% at 6 kW. Methodology applied at pilot scale with the same result; informed procurement of automatic tuners.
